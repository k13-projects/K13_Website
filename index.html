04 Working together

It starts with a connection.

I don't take a project to fill a calendar. I take it because something in it grabs me — the problem, the people, the place. When that's there, I'll pour myself in and be proud to put my name on it. When it isn't, I'll tell you honestly and point you somewhere better.

i.

You care how it feels

The flip-clock, the flip-to-reveal, the re-priced coupon. The details are the product.

ii.

The problem is real

Someone's day gets better when this ships — a team, a kitchen, a city block. That's fuel I run on.

iii.

We talk like humans

Two honest sentences beat twenty pages of requirements. If the first call feels like a conversation, we're building.

No long form. A few taps, in your own words — what you're dreaming up and why it matters to you. That's genuinely what I read for. You'll hear back within a day, honest thoughts either way.
